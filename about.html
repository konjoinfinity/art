About Konjo



Konjo started painting in September 2018. He has an intense experimental approach utilizing multiple mediums including spray, acrylic, oil, gouache, on canvas, foamboard, cardboard, etc.

Konjo is inspired by beauty, discovery, passion, in a healthy & positive universe. He hopes that his paintings fill people with emotion and arouse feelings not otherwise felt.

Other themes explored through painting include:

Freedom
Movement
Determination
Elegance
Perfection
